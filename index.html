How to Play

Objective

Catch your opponent's meeple, collect 3 Codebreakers to win, or avoid collecting 3 Daredevils (which makes you lose).

Your Turn

  1. Play: Select 2 cards with different names from your hand
  2. Choose: Pick which card to play face-up (the other is face-down)
  3. Recruit: Your opponent picks one card - you get the other
  4. Move: Move your meeple based on the recruited card's movement icons

Movement Icons

Each card has 3 movement values. Which one you use depends on how many copies of that agent you've recruited:

  • 1 copy ‚Üí Use 1st movement value
  • 2 copies ‚Üí Use 2nd movement value
  • 3+ copies ‚Üí Use 3rd movement value
